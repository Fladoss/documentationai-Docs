---
title: How do I start using Cuddly Records?
description: Basic instructions for new users.
---

## First Steps

1. Sign up at `https://cuddlyrecords.com`.
2. Confirm your email address.
3. Create your first project from the dashboard.

## Adding a Record

1. Click "Add Record" in your project window.
2. Fill in the fields: Name, Artist, Genre.
3. Save to store your record in Cuddly Records.
