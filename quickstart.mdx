## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- No prior installation required - everything runs in your browser
</Callout>

## Sign Up for an Account

Follow these steps to create your Cuddly Records account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://app.cuddlyrecords.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Registration" icon="user-plus">
    Click the `Sign Up` button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, full name, and a secure password. Accept the terms of service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Cuddly Records and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In to Your Account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password" icon="mail">
    Enter your registered email and password on the login page. Click `Log In`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google SSO" icon="apple">
    Select `Continue with Google` and authorize the connection from your Google account.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings for added security after your first login.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Projects" icon="folder">
    Once logged in, you'll land on the dashboard. Click `New Project` in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Project" icon="settings">
    Enter a project name like `My First Docs`, select a template (e.g., `Documentation`), and choose your brand color `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project" icon="check-circle">
    Click `Create`. Your new documentation space opens automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
